# Post Angles

Pick one primary angle per post.

## Recommended angles

- `why_this_matters`: explain why the update matters now
- `winner_loser`: explain who benefits and who gets squeezed
- `workflow_change`: explain how the product changes daily work
- `market_signal`: explain what this says about the industry
- `overhyped_or_real`: judge whether the hype is justified
- `builder_take`: tell founders or creators what to do with the change

## Angle examples

### why_this_matters

Use when the news is important but the impact is not obvious.

Opening ideas:

- Most people are looking at the feature. The real story is the distribution shift.
- This looks like a product update, but it is really a pricing move.

### winner_loser

Use when the update clearly changes competition.

Opening ideas:

- The biggest winner from this launch may not be the company that announced it.
- This update makes life easier for users and harder for smaller competitors.

### workflow_change

Use when people can apply the tool right away.

Opening ideas:

- This is the first AI update in a while that actually changes daily workflow.
- If you use AI tools every day, this is the part worth paying attention to.

### market_signal

Use when the item reflects a larger platform trend.

Opening ideas:

- This is less about one launch and more about where the market is moving.
- The update itself is fine. The strategic signal is the real takeaway.

### overhyped_or_real

Use when public reaction is strong or polarized.

Opening ideas:

- This is getting a lot of hype, but only one part really matters.
- I do not think this changes everything, but it does change one important thing.

### builder_take

Use when the audience includes founders, operators, and creators.

Opening ideas:

- If I were building in AI right now, I would pay attention to this move.
- Builders should read this as a distribution signal, not just a feature release.
